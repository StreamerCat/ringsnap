### Temperature-Based Urgency

```
Treat as high priority requiring same-day or emergency:
- No AC when outside temp is 90°F+ 
- No heat when outside temp is below 32°F
- Carbon monoxide detector going off (emergency transfer!)
- Burning smell from furnace (emergency transfer)
- Gas smell (emergency transfer)
```

## Seasonal Strategies

### Summer (AC Season)

Update instructions when AC season hits:

```
Summer rush is here! For AC not cooling:
- Ask when it stopped working and outside temperature
- Ask about system age
- Prioritize homes with elderly, infants, or medical needs
- Mention our same-day service for emergencies
- Book maintenance for systems that just need tune-ups
```

### Winter (Heating Season)

```
Heating season priorities:
- No heat with temps below freezing = priority or emergency
- Ask if they have alternative heating (space heaters)
- Mention our 24/7 emergency heating repair
- Furnace inspections available for prevention
```

### Spring/Fall (Maintenance Season)

```
Great time for system tune-ups! 
Offer seasonal maintenance packages.
Ask when their system was last serviced.
Book maintenance appointments before the rush.
```

HVAC pricing is complex. Here's guidance:

```
For pricing questions, we provide free estimates because every job differs.

- Diagnostic / service call: $89-149
- AC tune-up: $79-129
```

Train your AI to ask about system age:

```
"Do you know approximately how old your system is?"

"That's getting to the age where repair vs. replace is worth discussing.

Let's get it repaired and running efficiently."
```
