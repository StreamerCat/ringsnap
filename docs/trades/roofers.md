## Insurance Claim Assistance

A major value-add for homeowners:

### What Your AI Should Say

```
For insurance-related calls:

"We work with all insurance companies and can help with the claims process. 
Our inspection includes detailed documentation with photos that insurance companies accept.

Have you filed a claim yet? [Capture Y/N]
If yes, what's your claim number? [Capture if provided]
Who is your insurance company? [Capture]

We can meet with your adjuster if needed."
```

### Capturing Claim Details

```
For insurance claims, gather:
- Insurance company name
- Claim number (if they have one)
- Has adjuster visited yet? Y/N
- Adjuster visit date (if scheduled)
- Property address
```

## Inspection Scheduling

### Recommended Inspection Blocks

| Inspection Type | Block Time |
|----------------|------------|
| Storm/hail inspection | 45 min |
| Full replacement estimate | 1-2 hours |
| Leak investigation | 1 hour |
| Insurance adjuster meeting | 1-2 hours |

### What to Capture for Inspections

```
For roof inspections, ask:
- Property address
- Approximate roof age if known
- Type of roofing (shingles, tile, metal)
- Is there damage visible from the ground?
- When was the last storm/hail in their area?
- One-story or two-story home?
```
