# Getting Started with RingSnap

Welcome to RingSnap — the AI-powered answering service that sounds human and never misses a call. This guide will walk you through setting up your AI receptionist in about 10 minutes.

---

## What is RingSnap?

RingSnap is an AI receptionist built specifically for home service contractors (plumbers, HVAC technicians, electricians, roofers, landscapers, and more). Unlike traditional answering services, RingSnap:

- **Answers in under 1 second** — before your competition
- **Sounds completely human** — customers can't tell it's AI
- **Books appointments automatically** — directly to your calendar
- **Works 24/7/365** — even on holidays and after hours
- **Handles emergencies** — routes critical calls to you immediately

---

## Quick Setup (10 Minutes)

### Step 1: Start Your Free Trial

1. Visit [getringsnap.com/start](https://www.getringsnap.com/start)
2. Enter your email address and name
3. You'll receive a verification email — click the link to continue

### Step 2: Complete Onboarding

Our AI-guided onboarding will ask you a few questions to personalize your receptionist:

| Information Needed | Why We Ask |
|-------------------|------------|
| **Phone Number** | Where to forward calls if needed |
| **Company Name** | How your AI introduces itself |
| **Trade/Service** | Tailors responses to your industry |
| **Website** (optional) | Helps personalize caller interactions |
| **Business Hours** | Knows when you're available vs. after-hours |
| **Voice Preference** | Choose male or female voice |
| **Primary Goal** | Book appointments, capture leads, answer questions, or take orders |

### Step 3: Add Payment Method

- Enter your card details for the 3-day free trial
- You get **150 minutes** of call time during your trial
- **No charge until your trial ends**
- Cancel anytime with no penalty

### Step 4: Forward Your Phone Number

After signup, you'll receive a dedicated RingSnap phone number. Simply forward your business line to it:

#### Common Carrier Instructions

| Carrier | To Enable | To Disable |
|---------|-----------|------------|
| **AT&T** | Dial `*21*[RingSnap#]*` | Dial `#21#` |
| **Verizon** | Dial `*72` + [RingSnap#] | Dial `*73` |
| **T-Mobile** | Dial `**21*[RingSnap#]#` | Dial `##21#` |
| **Other/Landline** | Contact your provider | Contact your provider |

> **💡 Tip:** You keep your existing phone number. Customers call your same number, and it forwards to RingSnap automatically.

### Step 5: Test Your AI Receptionist

1. Call your business number
2. Listen to how your AI greets callers
3. Try booking an appointment or asking a question
4. Check your dashboard to see the call log

**That's it — you're live!** 🎉

## Your Dashboard

Once set up, your customer dashboard gives you full control:

### Today Tab
- View calls received today
- See real-time activity

### Overview Tab
- Usage statistics and minutes remaining
- Recent activity log
- Quick stats on call performance

### Phone Numbers Tab
- View your RingSnap phone number
- Manage call forwarding settings
- View provisioning status

### Assistants Tab
- Customize your AI receptionist's behavior
- Review and edit configurations

### Team Tab
- Invite team members to your account
- Manage user roles

### Settings Tab
- **Custom Instructions**: Tell your AI specific things to mention (e.g., "Always mention our 24/7 emergency service")
- **Business Details**: Update website, service area, emergency policy, and business hours
- **Call Recording**: Enable/disable call recording (Professional+ plans)
- **SMS Settings**: Configure appointment confirmations and reminders

### Billing Tab
- View your current plan and usage
- Update payment method
- View billing history and invoices
- Upgrade or cancel your subscription

## How It Works

```
Customer calls your number
        ↓
Call forwards to RingSnap
        ↓
AI answers in < 1 second
        ↓
Handles the conversation naturally
        ↓
Books appointment OR captures lead OR transfers emergency
        ↓
You get notified immediately
```

### Emergency Handling

Life-threatening emergencies are **always transferred to you immediately** (typically in under 5 seconds). The AI provides full context before the transfer so you know exactly what you're walking into.
