## Understanding Minutes

### How Minutes Are Calculated

- Minutes are calculated per **call duration**
- Measured in actual talk time (not connection time)
- Rounded to the nearest minute per call

### Typical Usage

| Business Type | Avg Call Length | Calls/Day | Monthly Minutes |
|---------------|-----------------|-----------|-----------------|
| Solo Operator | 3-4 min | 10-15 | 900-1,800 |
| Small Team (2-3) | 3-4 min | 20-30 | 1,800-3,600 |
| Multi-Location | 3-4 min | 40-60 | 3,600-7,200 |

### Overage Charges

If you exceed your included minutes:
- Overages are billed at the end of your billing cycle
- Rates depend on your plan (see table above)
- You'll receive a notification when you hit 80% usage

## Managing Your Subscription

### Viewing Your Current Plan

**Dashboard → Billing → Current Plan**

Shows:
- Plan name and price
- Minutes used / included
- Next billing date
- Subscription status

### Upgrading Your Plan

1. Go to **Dashboard → Billing**
2. Click **Change Plan** or **Upgrade Now**
3. Select your new plan
4. Confirm the upgrade

**Upgrade Details:**
- Takes effect immediately
- Pro-rated for the current billing cycle
- New minutes available instantly

### Downgrading Your Plan

1. Go to **Dashboard → Billing**
2. Click **Change Plan**
3. Select a lower tier
4. Confirm the change

**Downgrade Details:**
- Takes effect at the end of your current billing cycle
- Keep current features until then
- Unused minutes don't roll over

### Accepted Payment Types

### Updating Your Card

1. Go to **Dashboard → Billing → Payment Method**
2. Click **Update**
3. Enter new card details
4. Click **Save**

### Failed Payments

If a payment fails:
1. You'll receive an email notification
2. We'll retry the payment after 1-3 days
3. Update your card to avoid service interruption
4. After multiple failures, service may be paused

## Cancellation

### During Trial

1. Go to **Dashboard → Billing**
2. Click **Cancel Trial**
3. Confirm cancellation

**Result:**
- Immediate cancellation
- No charges to your card
- Phone number released

### Active Subscription

1. Go to **Dashboard → Billing**
2. Scroll to **Cancel Subscription**
3. Click **Cancel Subscription**
4. Confirm cancellation

**Result:**
- Subscription remains active until billing period ends
- No additional charges after current period
- Phone number released at period end

### What Happens to My Data?

- Call logs retained for 30 days after cancellation
- Recordings deleted after 30 days
- Account can be reactivated within 30 days

## Frequently Asked Billing Questions

### When am I charged?

- **Trial**: No charge for 3 days
- **Subscription**: Billed on the same day each month as your signup date

### Can I switch plans mid-cycle?

- **Upgrades**: Immediate, pro-rated
- **Downgrades**: Effective at next billing cycle

### Do unused minutes roll over?

No, unused minutes don't roll over to the next month. Choose a plan that matches your expected usage.

### Can I pause instead of cancel?

Currently, we don't offer pause functionality. You can cancel and resubscribe at any time.

### Are there annual discounts?

Contact sales@getringsnap.com for annual pricing options.

### Is there a setup fee?

No. All plans have zero setup fees.

### What if I need more than Premium?

Contact sales@getringsnap.com for enterprise pricing and custom solutions.
