# Customizing Your AI Receptionist

This guide covers all the ways you can personalize your RingSnap AI receptionist to match your business perfectly.

---

## Quick Overview

Your AI receptionist can be customized in several ways:

| Setting | Location | Effect |
|---------|----------|--------|
| Custom Instructions | Settings Tab | Tell AI what to say/emphasize |

## Custom Instructions

### What Are Custom Instructions?

Custom instructions let you tell your AI specific things to mention, questions to ask, or behaviors to follow. They're like giving a new employee a quick briefing.

### Where to Set Them

**Dashboard → Settings → AI Assistant Customization**

### Best Practices

✅ **Do:**
- Be specific and clear
- Focus on your unique value props
- Include promotions or special offers
- Mention certifications or warranties

❌ **Don't:**
- Write entire scripts (the AI handles natural conversation)
- Use jargon the AI might misinterpret
- Exceed 500 characters

### Example Instructions

#### Plumber
```
Always mention we offer 24/7 emergency service with no extra charge. 
Ask if they're experiencing any water damage and prioritize those calls.
We're licensed, bonded, and insured with 25+ years experience.
```

#### HVAC
```
Mention our same-day service for AC emergencies during summer.
Ask about the age of their system - anything 15+ years, suggest a free evaluation.
We offer financing on new installations.
```

#### Electrician
```
Always ask if it's a safety issue (sparks, burning smell, flickering).
Prioritize panel upgrades and EV charger installs - those are our specialty.
Mention we're fully licensed and pull all required permits.
```

#### Roofer
```
Ask if they've noticed any water stains on ceilings or walls.
Mention we offer free inspections after storms.
Emphasize we're GAF certified and offer workmanship warranties.
```

## Business Hours

### Setting Your Hours

**Dashboard → Settings → Business Details → Edit Hours**

### Preset Options

| Option | Hours |
|--------|-------|
| **Weekdays 9-5** | Mon-Fri 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM |
| **Every Day 8-6** | Mon-Sun 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM |
| **24/7** | Always available |
| **Custom** | Set each day individually |

### How Hours Affect Calls

- **During business hours**: AI mentions you're open, can offer callbacks
- **After hours**: AI acknowledges it's after hours, can still book or capture leads
- **Emergencies**: Always transferred regardless of hours

## Emergency Policy

### What to Include

Your emergency policy tells the AI how to handle urgent situations:

```
For gas leaks, flooding, or electrical emergencies, immediately transfer 
to my cell: (303) 555-1234. For other urgent but non-emergency issues, 
schedule them for the next available slot and text me the details.
```

### Categories to Consider

| Category | Handling |
|----------|----------|
| **Life Safety** (gas, fire, electric) | Immediate transfer |
| **Property Damage** (active flooding) | Immediate transfer or same-day |
| **Urgent Comfort** (no heat in winter) | Priority booking |
| **Standard** (dripping faucet) | Normal booking |

## Service Area

### Setting Your Coverage

**Dashboard → Settings → Business Details → Service Area**

### How to Format

Be specific but natural:
- ✅ "Denver Metro Area including Aurora, Lakewood, and Littleton"
- ✅ "Within 30 miles of downtown Phoenix"
- ❌ "Colorado" (too broad)
- ❌ Zip code lists (hard for AI to use naturally)

### Out-of-Area Calls

When a caller is outside your service area, the AI will politely let them know and offer to take their information in case you expand service.

## Primary Goal

Your primary goal determines how the AI prioritizes conversations:

| Goal | AI Behavior |
|------|-------------|
| **Book Appointments** | Actively offers available times, confirms bookings |
| **Capture Leads** | Collects contact info, service needs, notifies you |
| **Answer Questions** | Provides information, FAQs, directs appropriately |
| **Take Orders** | Captures order details, confirms, sends summary |

## SMS Settings

### Appointment Confirmations

When enabled, callers who book appointments receive a confirmation text with:
- Appointment date and time
- Your company name
- Company phone number

### Reminder Messages

When enabled, appointment reminders are sent:
- 24 hours before
- 1 hour before (optional)

### Setting Up SMS

**Dashboard → Settings → SMS Settings**

Toggle on:
- Appointment Confirmations
- Reminder Messages

## Call Recording

### Availability

Call recording is available on **Professional and Premium plans**.

### Enabling Recording

1. Go to **Dashboard → Settings → Call Recording**
2. Toggle "Enable Call Recording"
3. Accept the consent dialog (required by law)

### State Consent Requirements

The consent dialog shows:
- Your state's recording laws
- Required notification text
- Whether you need one-party or two-party consent

Once accepted, the AI will include appropriate disclosure when recording.
