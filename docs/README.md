## About RingSnap

RingSnap answers calls in under 1 second, handles real conversations, follows your rules, and learns from outcomes. Built for:

- 🔧 Plumbers
- ❄️ HVAC Technicians  
- ⚡ Electricians
- 🏠 Roofers
- 🌿 Landscapers
- 🔨 General Contractors

### Key Features

- **Answer 95%+ of calls automatically**
- **24/7/365 availability** — even holidays
- **Book appointments directly** — integrates with Google Calendar
- **Human-sounding AI** — customers can't tell the difference
- **Emergency handling** — critical calls transferred immediately
- **Call recording & transcripts** — review every conversation
- **Multi-language support** — English and Spanish
- **Enterprise security** — encrypted, isolated, compliant
