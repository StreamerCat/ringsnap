# Team Management Guide

Learn how to invite team members, manage roles, and collaborate on your RingSnap account.

---

## Overview

RingSnap allows you to invite team members to access your dashboard, view call logs, and manage settings. This is useful for:

- Office managers who handle calls
- Dispatchers who schedule appointments
- Business partners who need visibility
- Technicians who want to see their calls

---

## User Roles

| Role | Capabilities |
|------|-------------|
| **Owner** | Full access to everything including billing, user management, and account deletion |
| **Admin** | Full access to dashboard, settings, and user management (not billing) |
| **Member** | View-only access to calls and activity |

---

## Inviting Team Members

### Step-by-Step

1. Go to **Dashboard → Team**
2. Click **Invite Member**
3. Enter the team member's email address
4. Select their role (Admin or Member)
5. Click **Send Invite**

### What Happens Next

1. An email is sent to the invitee
2. They click the link to create their password
3. They can now log in and access the shared dashboard
4. You'll see them listed in the Team tab

---

## Managing Team Members

### Viewing Your Team

**Dashboard → Team**

Shows all team members with:
- Name
- Email
- Role
- Status (active, pending)
- Date added

### Changing a Member's Role

1. Find the member in the Team list
2. Click on their entry
3. Select a new role from the dropdown
4. Click **Save**

### Removing a Team Member

1. Find the member in the Team list
2. Click the remove/delete icon
3. Confirm the removal

**Note:** Removed members immediately lose access but can be re-invited later.

---

## Pending Invitations

### What Is a Pending Invitation?

When you invite someone, they appear as "Pending" until they accept the invitation by clicking the email link.

### Resending an Invitation

1. Go to **Dashboard → Team**
2. Find the pending invitation
3. Click **Resend Invite**

### Canceling an Invitation

1. Go to **Dashboard → Team**
2. Find the pending invitation
3. Click **Cancel** or the delete icon

## Best Practices

### Who to Invite as Admin

- Office managers
- Business partners
- Dispatchers who need to update settings

### Who to Invite as Member

- Field technicians
- Sales staff who need to review calls
- Anyone who needs read-only access

### Security Tips

- Remove team members promptly when they leave
- Use work email addresses, not personal
- Periodically review your team list

## Multi-Location Teams

If you have multiple business locations:

1. **Single Account**: All locations share one account and dashboard
2. **Separate Accounts**: Each location has its own account (recommended for independent operations)

For multi-location dashboard needs, contact support about **Premium** plans with multi-location features.

## Troubleshooting

### Team member didn't receive the invitation

1. Check they entered the correct email
2. Ask them to check spam/junk folders
3. Resend the invitation
4. Try a different email address

### Team member can't log in

1. Ensure they accepted the invitation first
2. Try the "Forgot Password" flow
3. Verify their email address is correct in your Team list

### I'm locked out and I'm the owner

1. Use the "Forgot Password" flow with your account email
2. If that doesn't work, contact support@getringsnap.com
