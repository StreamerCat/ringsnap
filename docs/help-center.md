## Frequently Asked Questions

### Will callers know they're talking to an AI?

No. RingSnap uses advanced voice technology that sounds completely natural. Customers typically can't tell they're speaking with AI. We offer premium voice cloning on Professional and Premium plans to match your team's voice exactly.

### What happens during emergencies (gas leak, electrical fire, etc.)?

Life-threatening emergencies are **always transferred to you immediately** — typically in under 5 seconds. The AI provides full context of the situation before the transfer so you know exactly what you're walking into.

### Can RingSnap handle Spanish-speaking customers?

Yes! Professional and Premium plans include multi-language support. The AI seamlessly switches between English and Spanish based on the customer's preference.

### Do I need to change my phone number?

No. You keep your existing phone number. Setup takes about 10 minutes — just forward your number to your RingSnap receptionist.

### Is there a contract or commitment?

No long-term contracts! You can cancel anytime. We want you to stay because you love the service, not because you're locked in.

### How long does setup take?

Most customers are live within **10 minutes**. Our AI-guided onboarding walks you through everything step by step.

### How many calls can RingSnap handle at once?

Unlike human receptionists, RingSnap can handle **unlimited simultaneous calls**. No caller will ever get a busy signal or go to voicemail because you're on another call.

## Phone Numbers & Forwarding

### How does call forwarding work?

When you sign up, you receive a dedicated RingSnap phone number. You forward your existing business line to this number. When customers call your business, the call automatically routes to RingSnap.

### How do I set up call forwarding?

#### AT&T
- **Enable**: Dial `*21*[Your RingSnap Number]*`
- **Disable**: Dial `#21#`

#### Verizon
- **Enable**: Dial `*72` then enter your RingSnap number
- **Disable**: Dial `*73`

#### T-Mobile
- **Enable**: Dial `**21*[Your RingSnap Number]#`
- **Disable**: Dial `##21#`

#### Landline/Other Providers
Contact your phone provider and request "unconditional call forwarding" to your RingSnap number.

### Can I keep my current business phone number?

Yes! RingSnap works alongside your existing number. Customers continue calling your normal business line — it just forwards to RingSnap automatically.

### What if I want to disable forwarding temporarily?

Use the disable codes above, or contact your phone carrier. Your RingSnap subscription remains active and ready when you re-enable forwarding.

### My phone number shows as "Provisioning"

Phone number provisioning typically takes 1-5 minutes after signup. If it takes longer than 15 minutes, please contact support.

## AI Receptionist Customization

### How do I customize what my AI says?

Go to **Dashboard → Settings → AI Assistant Customization**. Here you can add custom instructions like:

- "Always mention our 24/7 emergency service"
- "Offer a 10% discount for first-time customers"
- "Ask if they were referred by someone"

### Can I set my business hours?

Yes! Go to **Dashboard → Settings → Business Details** and click "Edit Hours." You can set different hours for each day of the week, or choose presets like "Weekdays 9am-5pm" or "24/7."

### How do I update my emergency policy?

Navigate to **Dashboard → Settings → Business Details** and fill in the "Emergency Policy" field. This tells your AI how to handle urgent calls.

### Can I update my service area?

Yes. Go to **Dashboard → Settings → Business Details** and update the "Service Area" field (e.g., "Denver Metro Area").

### How do I enable call recording?

Call recording is available on Professional and Premium plans. Go to **Dashboard → Settings → Call Recording** and toggle it on. You'll need to accept consent requirements based on your state's recording laws.

## Call Handling

### What happens when someone calls?

1. The call forwards to your RingSnap number
2. Your AI answers in under 1 second
3. The AI greets the caller and handles their request
4. Based on your configuration, the AI will:
   - Book an appointment
   - Capture lead information
   - Answer common questions
   - Transfer emergencies to you
5. You receive a notification with call details

### How are appointments booked?

RingSnap integrates with Google Calendar and Zapier. When a caller wants to book, the AI checks availability and schedules the appointment directly.

### What if the AI can't answer a question?

If the AI is uncertain or the caller has a complex request, it will:
- Collect all relevant details from the caller
- Set appropriate expectations
- Transfer or escalate based on your rules

### Can I listen to call recordings?

Yes (Professional and Premium plans). Call recordings are accessible in your dashboard under the call logs section.

### How do I see my call history?

Go to **Dashboard → Today** for today's calls, or **Dashboard → Overview** for historical data and statistics.

### What information is captured for each call?

- Caller phone number
- Call duration
- Transcript summary
- Outcome (appointment booked, lead captured, etc.)
- Timestamp

## Billing & Subscriptions

### What are the pricing plans?

| Plan | Monthly Price | Included Minutes | Overage Rate |
|------|--------------|------------------|--------------|
| **Starter** | $297 | 1,500 | $0.16/min |
| **Professional** | $547 | 3,500 | $0.13/min |
| **Premium** | $947 | 7,000 | $0.11/min |

### How does the free trial work?

- 3 days to try RingSnap completely risk-free
- Includes 150 minutes of call time
- Your card is not charged until after the trial ends
- Cancel anytime during the trial with no charge

### How do I upgrade my plan?

Go to **Dashboard → Billing → Change Plan** and select your new plan. Upgrades take effect immediately.

### How do I update my payment method?

Go to **Dashboard → Billing → Payment Method** and click "Update."

### How do I view my invoices?

Go to **Dashboard → Billing → Billing History** to view and download past invoices.

### What happens if I go over my included minutes?

Additional minutes are billed at your plan's overage rate at the end of your billing cycle:
- Starter: $0.16/minute
- Professional: $0.13/minute
- Premium: $0.11/minute

### How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to **Dashboard → Billing** and scroll to "Cancel Subscription." 

- **During trial**: Cancellation is immediate; your phone number is released
- **After trial**: Subscription remains active until the end of your billing period

### Can I pause my subscription instead of canceling?

Currently, we don't offer pause functionality. However, you can cancel and resubscribe at any time.

## Troubleshooting

### Calls aren't going to RingSnap

1. **Verify forwarding is active**: Call your business number from a different phone
2. **Check forwarding settings**: Ensure you used the correct RingSnap number
3. **Contact your carrier**: Verify unconditional forwarding is enabled
4. **Check provisioning status**: Your RingSnap number should show as "Active" in Dashboard → Phone Numbers

### My AI isn't following my custom instructions

- Custom instructions take effect on the **next call** after saving
- Ensure you clicked "Save Instructions" after making changes
- Instructions have a 500-character limit

### I can't log in

1. Ensure you're using the correct email address
2. Try resetting your password
3. Clear your browser cache and cookies
4. Try a different browser

### My dashboard shows no calls, but I know I received them

1. Verify your call forwarding is set up correctly
2. Check that calls are going to your RingSnap number, not your direct line
3. Allow a few minutes for call logs to sync
4. Contact support if the issue persists

### Recording isn't working

1. Ensure you have a Professional or Premium plan
2. Verify call recording is enabled in Settings
3. Check that you've accepted the recording consent dialog
