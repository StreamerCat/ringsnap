# Call Forwarding Setup Guide

This guide provides detailed instructions for forwarding your business phone number to RingSnap across different carriers and phone systems.

---

## How It Works

1. You keep your existing business phone number
2. Customers call your number as usual
3. The call automatically forwards to your RingSnap AI receptionist
4. RingSnap answers in under 1 second and handles the call
5. You receive notifications and can view call logs in your dashboard

---

## Your RingSnap Phone Number

After signing up, you'll receive a dedicated RingSnap phone number. You can find it in:
- Your welcome email
- **Dashboard → Phone Numbers**

This is the number you'll forward your calls to.

## Mobile Carrier Instructions

### AT&T

**Enable Unconditional Forwarding:**
```
Dial: *21*[RingSnap Number]*
Press: Call/Send
```

**Disable Forwarding:**
```
Dial: #21#
Press: Call/Send
```

**Example:** `*21*13035551234*`

---

### Verizon

**Enable Unconditional Forwarding:**
```
Dial: *72
Wait for dial tone
Dial: [RingSnap Number]
```

**Disable Forwarding:**

```
Dial: *73
```

## Business Phone Systems

### VoIP/Cloud Phone Systems

Most VoIP systems (RingCentral, Grasshopper, Nextiva, 8x8, etc.) have forwarding settings in their admin portal:

1. Log in to your phone system admin
2. Navigate to Call Forwarding or Call Handling
3. Select "Forward all calls" or "Unconditional forwarding"
4. Enter your RingSnap number
5. Save changes

### Traditional Landlines

Contact your local phone company (AT&T, CenturyLink, Frontier, etc.) and request:
- "Unconditional call forwarding" to your RingSnap number

Some landline carriers allow self-service:
```
Dial: *72[RingSnap Number]
Wait for: Two short beeps (confirmation)
```

To disable:
```
Dial: *73
Wait for: Confirmation tone
```

## Forwarding Options

### Unconditional Forwarding (Recommended)
All calls go directly to RingSnap. Best for 24/7 coverage.

### Conditional Forwarding
Forwards only under certain conditions:

| Condition | When to Use |
|-----------|-------------|
| **No Answer** | Ring X times first, then forward |
| **Busy** | Forward when you're on another call |
| **Unreachable** | Forward when phone is off/no signal |

**Note:** Conditional forwarding may add delays. For best caller experience, we recommend unconditional forwarding.

## Testing Your Setup

1. **Set up forwarding** using the instructions above
2. **Call your business number** from a different phone (not the one you forwarded)
3. **Verify RingSnap answers** — you should hear your AI receptionist
4. **Check your dashboard** — the call should appear in your call logs

## Forwarding Multiple Numbers

If you have multiple business numbers to forward:
- Forward each number to your **same RingSnap number**
- RingSnap can handle unlimited simultaneous calls
- All calls appear in one unified dashboard

## Troubleshooting

### Calls aren't forwarding

1. **Verify the forwarding code**: Try setting it up again
2. **Call from a different number**: Your own phone might not trigger forwarding
3. **Check for typos**: Ensure the RingSnap number is entered correctly
4. **Contact your carrier**: Some accounts have forwarding restrictions

### Forwarding only works sometimes

- You may have conditional forwarding instead of unconditional
- Some carriers limit forwarding on certain plan types
- Business features may need to be enabled on your account

### I hear a "number not in service" message

- Double-check your RingSnap number in the dashboard
- Ensure your RingSnap account is active (not in provisioning)
- Contact support if your number shows as "Provisioning" for more than 15 minutes

### How do I temporarily disable RingSnap?

Use the "Disable" code for your carrier:
- Calls will ring your phone directly again
- Your RingSnap subscription remains active
- Re-enable forwarding anytime with the "Enable" code
