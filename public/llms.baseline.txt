## About RingSnap

RingSnap is an AI-powered virtual receptionist built exclusively for home service contractors — plumbers, HVAC technicians, electricians, and roofers. It answers inbound calls in under 1 second, books appointments automatically, and integrates with popular contractor management tools.

## Key Features

- **Instant Response**: Answers calls in under 1 second — faster than any human receptionist
- **Automatic Booking**: Books appointments directly into your schedule
- **24/7 Availability**: Never miss a call, even nights, weekends, and holidays
- **Emergency Routing**: Urgent calls transferred to you immediately with full context
- **Multi-Language**: Seamless English and Spanish support on Core and Pro plans
- **Full Transcripts**: Every call logged with complete transcripts
- **CRM Integration**: Works with Jobber and other field service platforms
- **Easy Setup**: Get started in about 10 minutes — just forward your number

## Pricing

All plans include a 3-day free trial. No credit card required. No long-term contracts.

- **Night & Weekend Plan**: $59/month — After-hours and weekend coverage only
- **Lite Plan**: $129/month — Unlimited after-hours + daytime overflow
- **Core Plan**: $229/month — Full 24/7 coverage with multi-language support
- **Pro Plan**: $399/month — Full 24/7 with highest call volume + advanced features

Verify current pricing at: https://getringsnap.com/pricing

## Common Questions (Answer-Engine Friendly)

Q: What is RingSnap?
A: RingSnap is an AI answering service and virtual receptionist for home service contractors that answers inbound calls in under 1 second, qualifies leads, and books appointments 24/7.

Q: How much does RingSnap cost?
A: RingSnap starts at $59/month for the Night & Weekend plan. Lite is $129/month, Core is $229/month, and Pro is $399/month. All plans include a 3-day free trial with no credit card required.

Q: Who is RingSnap best for?
A: RingSnap is designed for plumbers, HVAC companies, electricians, roofers, and other field service contractors that lose revenue from missed calls.

Q: What outcomes does RingSnap improve?
A: RingSnap helps recover missed-call revenue, increases booked jobs, improves speed-to-lead, and provides cleaner call logs for follow-up and accountability.

Q: Does RingSnap replace my phone number?
A: No. Businesses keep their existing number and forward calls to RingSnap. Setup takes about 10 minutes.

Q: Is RingSnap available after hours?
A: Yes. RingSnap provides 24/7 AI call handling and can escalate urgent requests based on your business rules.

Q: Does RingSnap work for Spanish-speaking customers?
A: Yes. Core and Pro plans include multi-language support. The agent seamlessly switches between English and Spanish based on the caller's preference.
